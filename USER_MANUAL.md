## 1. What Expense Mate Does

Expense Mate helps you:

- Record **income**, **expenses**, and **transfers** between wallets
- Track balances across **cash, bank, cards**, and other wallet types
- See **charts and reports** of your spending habits
- Set **budgets** and get alerts when you are close to limits
- Save toward **goals** (emergency fund, vacation, house, etc.)
- Manage **bills** with due dates and recurring schedules
- Stay secure with optional **PIN** and **biometric lock**
- **Export** data as CSV/PDF and **backup/restore** your records

Your data is stored on your device first and syncs to the cloud when you are online.

## 2. First-Time Setup

### Step 1 — Open the app

When you launch Expense Mate for the first time, you will see a short **onboarding** walkthrough. Swipe through the screens to learn the basics.

### Step 2 — Create an account or sign in

Choose one of these options:

| Method | How |
|--------|-----|
| **Email** | Tap **Register**, enter your name, email, and password |
| **Google** | Tap **Continue with Google** |
| **Apple** | Tap **Continue with Apple** (iOS only) |

### Step 3 — Verify your email (email sign-up only)

If you registered with email, check your inbox and verify your address. The app will guide you to the verification screen until this is done.

### Step 4 — Start using the app

After sign-in, the app automatically:

- Creates default **categories** (Food, Transport, Salary, etc.)
- Creates a default **Cash** wallet
- Syncs your data when internet is available

You land on the **Home** dashboard and can add your first transaction right away.

## 3. Sign In & Account

### Sign in again later

Open the app → enter **email and password**, or use **Google / Apple**.

### Forgot password

1. On the login screen, tap **Forgot Password**
2. Enter your email
3. Check your inbox for a reset link

### Sign out

1. Tap the **Settings** icon (gear) on the Home screen  
   — or go to **Settings** from the app menu
2. Scroll down and tap **Sign Out**
3. Confirm when prompted

### Delete account

1. Go to **Settings → Profile**
2. Scroll to the bottom
3. Tap **Delete Account** and confirm  

> **Warning:** This permanently removes your account. Export a backup first if you want to keep your data.

## 5. Home Dashboard

The Home screen is your financial overview.

### What you see

- **Total balance** across all wallets
- **Income / Expense / Net** summary for the current period
- **Budget alerts** (if any budget is near or over limit)
- **Savings goals** progress (top active goals)
- **Upcoming bills** due soon
- **Charts:** weekly spending, income vs expense, top categories, cash flow trend
- **Recent transactions** (last few entries)

### Quick actions

Tap any of these shortcuts:

- **Income** — log money received
- **Expense** — log money spent
- **Transfer** — move money between wallets
- **Budgets** — manage spending limits
- **Goals** — manage savings targets
- **Bills** — manage recurring bills

### Pull to refresh

Swipe down on the Home screen to refresh charts and recent data.

## 6. Transactions

### Add a new transaction

1. Tap **+ Add** (Home or Transactions tab)
2. Choose type: **Income**, **Expense**, or **Transfer**
3. Fill in:
   - **Title** (e.g. "Grocery shopping")
   - **Amount**
   - **Wallet** (and **To Wallet** for transfers)
   - **Category** (not required for transfers)
   - **Payment method** (Cash, Card, Bank Transfer, etc.)
   - **Date**
   - **Note** (optional)
   - **Recurring** toggle (optional)
4. Tap **Save**

### Edit a transaction

1. Open **Transactions** tab
2. Tap the transaction you want to change
3. Update the fields and tap **Update**

### Search and filter

On the Transactions screen:

- Use the **search bar** to find by title
- Open **filters** to narrow by type, category, wallet, or date
- Change **sort order** (e.g. by date or amount)

### Delete a transaction

Swipe a transaction **left to right** (or use the delete option if shown) to remove it. Wallet balances update automatically.

## 7. Wallets

Wallets represent where your money lives: cash, bank accounts, cards, mobile banking, etc.

### View wallets

Tap the **Wallets** tab to see all wallets and their current balances.

### Add a wallet

1. Tap **+ Add Wallet**
2. Enter **name** (e.g. "Main Bank")
3. Choose **wallet type**
4. Set **initial balance**
5. Optionally mark as **default wallet** (used when adding new transactions)
6. Tap **Create Wallet**

### Default wallet

The default wallet is pre-selected when you add a new transaction. Only one wallet should be default at a time.

### Delete a wallet

Swipe the wallet card to delete. **Default wallets cannot be deleted** — set another wallet as default first.

> **Note:** Deleting a wallet does not delete past transactions linked to it.

## 8. Categories

Categories organize your income and expenses (e.g. Food, Rent, Salary).

## 10. Budgets

Budgets help you limit spending for a period or category.

### Create a budget

1. Open **Budgets** (Home quick action or Reports icon)
2. Tap **+ Add Budget**
3. Enter:
   - **Name** (e.g. "Monthly Food")
   - **Limit amount**
   - **Period** — Daily, Weekly, Monthly, or Yearly
   - **Category** (optional — leave as "All expenses" for a total budget)
   - **Alert threshold** (e.g. 80% — you get notified when reached)
4. Tap **Create Budget**

### Track progress

Each budget shows:

- Progress bar (spent vs limit)
- Amount spent and remaining
- **Warning** color when near limit
- **Red** when over budget

### Budget alerts on Home

When a budget hits its alert threshold or goes over limit, a **Budget Alerts** card appears on the Home dashboard.

### Delete a budget

Swipe the budget card left to delete.

## 11. Savings Goals

Goals help you save toward a target (emergency fund, vacation, car, etc.).

### Create a goal

1. Open **Goals**
2. Tap **+ Add Goal**
3. Enter **name**, **target amount**, and **goal type**
4. Optionally set a **target date**
5. Tap **Create Goal**

### Add money to a goal

1. Tap a goal to open **Goal Detail**
2. Enter **contribution amount**
3. Add an optional **note**
4. Tap **Add Contribution**

Your progress bar updates immediately. Contribution history is listed below.

### Completed goals

When saved amount reaches the target, the goal shows a **completed** check mark.

### Delete a goal

Swipe the goal card left to delete.

## 12. Bills & Reminders

Track recurring or one-time bills (rent, utilities, subscriptions).

### Add a bill

1. Open **Bills**
2. Tap **+ Add Bill**
3. Fill in:
   - **Title** (e.g. "Electricity")
   - **Amount**
   - **Due date**
   - **Wallet** and **Category** (optional — needed if you mark as paid with auto-transaction)
   - **Recurring** toggle and repeat schedule (Weekly / Monthly / Yearly)
4. Tap **Create Bill**

### Mark as paid

Tap the **check** icon on a bill:

- The bill is marked paid
- If wallet and category are set, an **expense transaction** is created automatically
- **Recurring bills** advance to the next due date instead of staying paid

### Status colors

| Color | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| Normal | Due later |
| Orange | Due within 3 days |
| Red | Overdue |

### Upcoming bills on Home

Bills due within 7 days appear in the **Upcoming Bills** section on Home.

### Delete a bill

Swipe the bill card left to delete.

## 15. Security (PIN & Biometric)

Protect the app when someone else has your phone.

### Enable PIN lock

1. Go to **Settings → PIN & Biometric**
2. Enter a **4-digit PIN** twice
3. Tap **Enable PIN Lock**

When PIN lock is on, the app locks when you switch away and asks for your PIN when you return.

### Enable biometric unlock

1. Enable **PIN lock** first (required)
2. Turn on **Biometric Unlock**
3. Confirm with fingerprint or Face ID

On the lock screen, tap **Use Biometric** instead of entering your PIN.

### Disable security

On the Security screen, tap **Remove PIN Lock**. This also disables biometric unlock.

## 16. Export & Backup

Go to **Settings → Export & Backup**.

### Export CSV

Exports all transactions as a spreadsheet file (.csv). Good for Excel or Google Sheets.

1. Tap **Export CSV**
2. Choose where to share or save (email, Drive, Files, etc.)

### Export PDF

Creates a printable report with:

- Income, expense, and net totals
- Full transaction table

1. Tap **Export PDF**
2. Share or save the file

### Create backup

Creates a **JSON backup file** containing:

- Transactions, wallets, categories
- Budgets, goals, bills, savings

1. Tap **Create Backup**
2. Save the file somewhere safe (cloud storage recommended)

### Restore backup

1. Tap **Restore Backup**
2. Read the warning — existing records with the same IDs will be **overwritten**
3. Pick your `.json` backup file
4. Wait for confirmation

> **Tip:** Create a backup before switching phones or reinstalling the app.

## 17. Working Offline & Sync

Expense Mate works **without internet**.

### Offline behavior

- Add, edit, and delete transactions, wallets, budgets, etc.
- All changes save to your device immediately
- Charts and reports use local data

### When you go back online

- Data **syncs automatically** to the cloud
- If you use the same account on another device, synced data appears there too

### Sync indicator

Items waiting to sync are marked internally as pending. You do not need to tap anything — sync runs in the background.

### Conflict handling

If the same record was changed on two devices, the version with the **latest update time** is kept.

## 18. Tips for Daily Use

1. **Log expenses immediately** — small daily entries keep reports accurate.
2. **Use one default wallet** for everyday spending to speed up data entry.
3. **Set 2–3 budgets** for your biggest categories (Food, Transport, Entertainment).
4. **Review Reports weekly** — the Monthly view shows where money actually goes.
5. **Add bills** for rent and subscriptions so you never miss a due date.
6. **Create a backup monthly** — Settings → Export & Backup → Create Backup.
7. **Enable PIN lock** if you share your device or want extra privacy.

## 19. FAQ & Troubleshooting

### I forgot my PIN. What do I do?

There is no PIN recovery inside the app. You must **sign out and sign back in**, then set a new PIN under Settings → Security. Your financial data remains tied to your account.

### My balance looks wrong

- Check all **wallets** — total balance is the sum of every wallet
- Look for **duplicate or missing transactions**
- Make sure **transfers** are recorded correctly (they move money between wallets, not out of the system)

### Charts show no data

- Add at least one **expense or income** transaction for the selected period
- On Reports, try switching the **period** (Daily / Weekly / Monthly / Yearly)
- Pull to refresh on Home

### Email verification stuck

1. Go to **Profile → Resend verification email**
2. Check spam folder
3. Tap **Refresh** on the verification screen after confirming

### Export or backup failed

- Ensure the app has **storage permission** (Android)
- Free up device storage
- Try again with a stable internet connection (for sharing only)

### App asks for PIN every time

This is expected when **PIN lock** is enabled. Use **biometric unlock** for faster access, or disable PIN in Settings → Security.

### Data not syncing to another device

- Confirm both devices use the **same account**
- Check **internet connection** on both devices
- Open the app and wait a few seconds for background sync
- Pull to refresh on Home
